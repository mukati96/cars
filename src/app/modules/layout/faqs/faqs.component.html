FAQ's

Q: Is POST a dealership?

Yes. POST (PostYourCars Inc.) is a licensed DMV (Department of Motor Vehicles) dealership, meaning we have met all requirements to purchase / retail vehicles to or from private parties. POST is also a bonded dealer, guaranteeing that we adhere to all state laws, statues, and regulations the DMV sets forth.

Q: Why should I sell my car with PostYourCars?

There are numerous value propositions to selling your car with us. First off, you maintain possession of your car during the entire sales process. You do not have to leave it at a dealership. You are able to drive the car freely as you wish. Since your car is going to be listed online, we will open up the market for your car to every individual car buyer in the United States. Expanding your buying audience results in quicker sell times and higher priced retail offers. We also offer financing and leasing options to your buyer as most Americans require some form of financing in order to acquire a vehicle. PostYourCars engages in proprietary industry leading targeted marketing to ensure that we will get enough qualified eyeballs on your car listing to get your car sold at the highest market prices in the shortest amount of time. You will have a dedicated POST sales representative at your disposal to answer any questions you may have during the sales process and someone with the sales tools needed to maximize performance. And once your car is sold, we handle all the paperwork (DMV, Title, Finance) involved to complete the sale and transfer. There are no fees to list your car on our platform.

Q: How do I sell my car?

You can sell your vehicle entirely online through the POST platform and have it picked up once it has been sold. How does it work?

  1. Start by getting your free vehicle appraisal by chatting with our online specialist. We will ask you for your vehicle’s VIN #, mileage, and any extra features, then shortly send you an appraisal. This appraisal will show you different options you may want to consider such as current used car market conditions, your risk profile, and desired timeframe to sell the car.
  2. Get us photos of your vehicle. PostYourCars cannot stress the importance of having great looking photos of your vehicle. Most people will be buying your car sight unseen so spectacular photos are instrumental in selling your car quickly and at a favorable selling price. And the opposite is usually true as well – poor subpar photos will delay your vehicle’s sale and result in low-ball offers you will probably reject.
    We offer a POST+ service with professional photography for only $125 where you pay the photographer directly. This is highly recommended for luxury vehicles or anyone wanting to sell their car at retail top dollar. A prequalified automotive photographer will come to your location and shoot the car – simple, fast, and convenient. If you think you are a great photographer and would like to supply your own vehicle photos you as the seller have that option too.
  3. Your car listing goes live , after steps 1 & 2 have been completed. PostYourCars actively markets your car for sale online nationwide through PostYourCars.com and other automotive and social media websites. Our sales team fields all buyer inquiries for your vehicle and once we get an offer on your car our sales team will reach out to you to discuss next steps.
  4. You get paid immediately once the car is sold and in our possession.. We handle all DMV, title transfer, and finance/lease paperwork for you and for the buyer so you won’t have to step foot into any DMV office, car dealership, or bank. Let us do all the work for you.

Q: When do I get paid?

POST operates like other licensed DMV car dealerships regarding payment. Once your vehicle is in our possession, payment will be sent out to you via bank wire or check.

Q: How will I get proof that ownership of my vehicle has been transferred?

A copy of the DMV Bill of Sale and a Notice of Transfer and Release of Liability will be provided to you upon completion of your vehicle’s sale.

Q: What documentation is required from me to sell my car?

Our POST team will ask you to provide a copy of your driver’s license and a picture of your current odometer reading.

If you own the car outright, we will also need:
Picture of the Title (front and back) and DMV Registration. All 4 corners of the document must be visible in the picture.

If you have a lien on your vehicle:
Copy of your DMV Registration with lien holder information and your vehicle’s 10-day payoff statement.

Q: Is there a fee for selling my car?

PostYourCars withholds 4% of the car’s final selling price as our fee. If your car does not sell, there is no fee. List your car on our platform for free and only pay if it gets sold by us. Pick-up of your vehicle is free too.

Q: What can I expect during the POST selling process?

After your car has been listed live for sale, there is really nothing else for you to do in the meantime as our team takes over. Let us do what we do best – selling cars. You can just sit back and relax. A designated sales team professional will reach out to you after we have received a serious inquiry on your vehicle or if we have any suggestions on how we can increase the activity / leads on your vehicle listing. If at any time you have questions you can reach out to your designated PostYourCars sales professional.

Q: What preparations do I need to do for selling my car?

We will need great looking pictures of your vehicle so thoroughly vacuum and clean your car and remove all personal belongings inside the car and trunk before attempting to get the car photographed. If you have any valuable service records of the car you would like to hand over to the buyer, please gather them and put them in the glove compartment.

Q: Am I obligated to sell my car if I get an offer?

No. You are the vehicle owner and the decision maker. Any offers received on your vehicle are communicated directly to you and you can either reject, counter offer, or accept them.

Q: Will POST come to pick up my car?

Q: How do I know the car I am buying is in great condition?

All POST+ vehicles are verified by our photographers to ensure that the car photos shown in the PostYourCars listings are real photographs of the car you are buying. A current CarFax report is available for you to view which shows the car’s service history and accident report history (if any).
We also can help you coordinate a Pre-Purchase Inspection (PPI) done on the vehicle. A hired licensed mechanic will physically go out and conduct a thorough 150-point inspection and test-drive, and send back a full report for you to review. It is imperative that you buy with confidence.
