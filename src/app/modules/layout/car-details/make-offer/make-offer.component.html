Post Market Value Info

The POST Market Value (PMV) is PostYourCar’s estimated retail price for a car based on a detailed analysis of comparable current and previously sold cars as well as verified purchase offers. Our analysis takes into account specific details including premium options & packages, year, greater availability, demand, and condition. PostYourCar’s PMV should be used as a guide for making an educated offer decision. PostYourCar strives to provide a full transparent car purchasing experience.


FAQ

Question:

My bid is lower than the highest bid. Can my offer still be accepted?

Answer:

Absolutely! The highest offer doesn’t always get accepted for a variety of reasons (ie. Is the buyer paying all cash or is the buyer requiring financing? Does the buyer have a trade-in vehicle that needs to be sold as well? How many times has the buyer backed out of a highest bid?). The generated win rate percentage takes into account the potentiality that things like those may happen. Technically speaking, if every highest offer was accepted 100% of the time, any offers lower than the highest offer would have a win ratio of 0%.
